Education

Training the next generation of leaders

SickKids is internationally recognized for its high-quality educational programs. These programs provide cutting-edge training in subjects related to paediatric ophthalmology to various levels of medical trainees, as well as to non-ophthalmologist practitioners. Our mandate also extends to the education of parents and families struggling with eye disorders, both in the clinical setting and through our support groups.

Our educational programs include several initiatives that integrate research and clinical practice, with an emphasis on translational research that drives new treatments for patients.

Our Fellowships

Our program in Pediatric Ophthalmology and Strabismus has trained well over 100 fellows since its inception more than 40 years ago, and is accredited by the Association of University Professors of Ophthalmology. We train four fellows every year, making it one of the world’s largest fellowship programs focused on vision sciences.
