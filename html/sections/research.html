Research

The foundation of innovation care

The SickKids DOVS is internationally recognized for our cutting-edge research. Our high-impact research programs allow for rapid translation of innovative methods to the bedside, and provide unparalleled research training opportunities for residents, fellows, and graduates students from local, national and international institutions.

Research programs include

  • Retonoblastoma
  • Telemedicine and Retinopathy of Prematurity
  • NeuroVision
  • Ocular Genetics
  • Child Eye Care Advocacy
  • Surgical Innovation
