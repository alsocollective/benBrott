Discovery

A home is your personal space, your zen.

We want to know all of your high level requirements. ie.“We need 3 bedrooms on the upper floor” or “ we want our executive offices to have level requirements that provide us with a starting point. In addition, having a detailed discussion about your budget and schedule are crucial in the development of a realistic project plan.

Design

Let’s dive deeper into specifics of your project.

During this phase we dive deeper into design, keeping your budget top of mind as we further refine your vision. Everything from architectural plans, floor plans, elevations and material selections will be hammered out. This is often our clients favourite part, since you will get to see the first glimpse of what your future space will look and feel like. All this detail provides us with the information needed to prepare a detailed and customized construction budget and schedule

Reporting

We keep an open line of communication.

Transparency is one of our core values. We believe keeping an open line of communication with you is fundamental to the success of your project. Throughout the entire project (design to completion) you will have 24hr access to the schedule and daily logs. You will also receive a weekly invoice and budget update indicating where costs have been incurred and the balance required until project completion. Attached to each budget update is a copy of all invoices for that week. This allows you to review every financial details keeping the entire process transparent.

Feasibility

Time is money. We take both of yours seriously.

After we have spent time getting to know you and your proposed project, we will create a feasibility plan. This plan outlines the high level scope of work, schedule and budget discussed during discovery. During this meeting we will present both a preliminary design and construction budget as well as the design and construction agreement. This is a significant investment for clients and it is imperative that we respect your budget and set realistic expectations around it.

Constructing

We are there every step of the way.

Now we are ready to start construction based on your finalized design, scope of work, schedule and construction budget. We start by securing trades and suppliers up front to guarantee they are committed to your project budget and timeline. We perform quality assurance. We are there every step of the way to manage sub-contractors to ensure they have everything they need to complete their tasks as planned.

Finishing

The devil is in the details.

Everyone knows the saying and we couldn’t agree more. At this point, the project is substantially completed. We will arrange an on site walk through with you to create a completion list. This guarantees we have completed every last detail to your requirements. No detail is too small.
